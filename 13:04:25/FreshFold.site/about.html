About FreshFold

Our story, our mission, and our commitment to quality

Our Story

FreshFold began in 2015 with a simple idea: make quality dry cleaning services more convenient for busy professionals and families.

After years of struggling to find time to drop off and pick up clothes at the dry cleaner, our founder Jessica Chen decided there had to be a better way. She launched FreshFold with just one van and a partnership with a local dry cleaner.

Today, we operate our own state-of-the-art cleaning facility and a fleet of delivery vehicles serving communities across the region, but our mission remains the same: to provide exceptional cleaning with unmatched convenience.

Our Mission

At FreshFold, our mission is to make life easier for our customers while providing the highest quality garment care possible.

We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best, without sacrificing valuable time in their day. That's why we've built our entire business model around convenience, quality, and exceptional service.

Every garment we process is treated with the utmost care, using the most appropriate cleaning methods to ensure longevity and a pristine finish.

Our Eco-Friendly Commitment

Water Conservation

Our facility uses high-efficiency machines that reduce water usage by up to 40% compared to traditional dry cleaners.

Green Solvents

We've invested in eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are gentler on fabrics and better for the environment.

Recyclable Materials

From hangers to packaging, we prioritize recyclable and biodegradable materials in all aspects of our business.
