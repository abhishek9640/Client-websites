About FreshFold

Our story, our mission, and our commitment to quality

Our Story

FreshFold is a professional dry cleaning and laundry delivery service that started in 2015 with a simple thought: busy professionals and families need quality dry cleaning services and they deserve it in a convenient package.

AAfter years of rushing laundry to and from the dry cleaners, our founder Jessica Chen came to the conclusion that there had to be a better system. She started FreshFold with a single van and a dry cleaner partnership.

Now, we run our own, modern cleaning plant, as well as a fleet of delivery cars serving communities all over the area, yet our mission stays true: exceptional cleaning, unmatched convenience.

Our Mission

At FreshFold; we provide the highest level of garment care with added ease to our customers lives.

Discovering and sharing the belief that every individual should be able to look and feel is valuable as possible, without the compromise of valuable time in an individual day. Why we’ve constructed our entire business model around convenience, quality, and service.

We take care no matter how nice of a garment, we use the correct cleaning processes to make your pieces last, and look their best.

Our Eco-Friendly Commitment

Water Conservation

We use high efficiency equipment that reduces water consumption up to 40% over a traditional dry cleaner.

Green Solvents

We invested in eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are less harsh on fabrics and have a softer impact on the environment.

Recyclable Materials

From hangers to the packaging, we use recyclable and biodegradable materials in all areas of business.
