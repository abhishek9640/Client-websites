Our Story

Founded in 2018, PetGroomer.site started with a simple mission: to provide convenient, stress-free grooming services for pets in the comfort of their own homes.

Our founder, Jane Smith, recognized that many pets experience anxiety when visiting traditional grooming salons. By bringing professional services directly to pet owners' homes, we've created a more relaxed experience for pets and their owners.

Today, we're proud to serve communities across the United States with our team of certified, experienced groomers who share our passion for pet care and well-being.

Our Team

Our team consists of certified professional groomers with years of experience working with all breeds and temperaments. Each team member undergoes rigorous training and stays up-to-date with the latest grooming techniques and pet care practices.
