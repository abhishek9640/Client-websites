Graffiti Removal

Our primary service removes unwanted graffiti using specialized techniques and products tailored to each specific surface. We work on:

  • Stone surfaces
  • Metal surfaces

  • Painted surfaces
  • Wood and plastic materials

Our technicians carefully assess each case to determine the most effective and least invasive removal method, ensuring your property is restored without damage.

Pressure Washing

Our high-quality pressure washing services effectively clean large areas and remove stubborn graffiti. This method is ideal for:

  • Concrete walls and walkways
  • Building exteriors
  • Parking lots and garages
  • Fences and barriers
  • Driveways and sidewalks

We use professional-grade equipment with adjustable pressure settings to ensure safe and effective cleaning for different surface types. Our process removes not only graffiti but also general grime and dirt, leaving your property looking fresh and renewed.

Anti-Graffiti Coatings

Prevent future graffiti damage with our protective coating services. These coatings create a barrier that makes graffiti removal faster, easier, and less expensive. Benefits include:

  • Sacrificial coatings that can be washed away with the graffiti
  • Permanent coatings that allow easy cleaning of graffiti
  • Clear, non-yellowing formulations
  • Protection for multiple surfaces (brick, concrete, metal, etc.)
  • Reduced cleaning costs over time

Our protective coatings are an excellent investment for properties in high-risk areas or those with a history of graffiti problems. They minimize damage and reduce long-term maintenance costs.
