About GreenSpray

Our narrative

GreenSpray started with a basic observation: too many gardens suffered from either overuse of water or neglect. Established in 2015 by a group of enthusiastic gardeners and water conservation experts, our goal was to develop a service that would tend to plants while honouring the most valuable resource on Earth.

Originally a little business catering our neighbourhood, it has developed into a reliable name in environmentally friendly lawn maintenance all around. Our dedication is still the same: enabling gardens to flourish with just the correct volume of water given in the most effective manner.

Our Commitment

Ecology

Every solution we create seeks to maximise water economy and reduce waste. Garden beauty shouldn't have to sacrifice environmental sustainability, in our opinion.

Expert Knowledge

Combining decades of knowledge in horticulture, irrigation design, and sustainable landscaping, our team provides solutions specifically for every garden.

Creative Originality

To offer the most effective solutions, we constantly investigate and include the newest water-saving technology and methods into our offerings.

Our Method of Approach

1

Consultation & Assessment

We start by closely looking over your garden, noting plant varieties, soil conditions, light exposure, and current watering systems.

2

Custom Solution Design

With an eye on water efficiency and plant health, our professionals design a customised watering schedule that fits the particular demands of your garden.

3

Monitoring and Implementing

After properly installing and calibrating your system, we give continuous assistance to alter as seasons vary and your garden expands.
