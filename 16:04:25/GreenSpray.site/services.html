Our Services

Tailored garden watering solutions for every need

Every garden should, in our opinion at GreenSpray, receive individual attention. While giving water conservation and plant health top priority, our range of services is made to satisfy the particular needs of many gardens and lifestyles.

Drip watering

Our drip irrigation systems maximise absorption by delivering water straight to plant roots, therefore reducing evaporation and runoff. Gardens including a variety of plant species, vegetable patches, and regions where water saving is a top concern will find this exact watering approach perfect.

Advances:

  • Uses less water than conventional sprinklers, up to 60%
  • Targets water just where necessary to minimise weed growth.
  • stops nutrient flow and soil erosion.
  • can be set for best efficiency and automated.
