About Us

Learn more about TurboShine and our commitment to excellence

Our Story

TurboShine started its operations in 2015, with one simple mission - to deliver car wash and detailing services that surpass customer expectations. From its humble beginnings, it has now become a name with confidence.

It all started when our founder Mike Johnson noticed the poor quality of car wash services available in the region. Having spent years in the automotive industry, he knew there had to be a better way to deliver a service which balanced speed with quality and attention to detail.

TurboShine continues to expand today, but our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction is still as strong as when we were first founded. We service every one of our vehicles with the utmost care, just like we would with our own.

Our Values

Quality

Quality is our mantra, we never compromise with it. Every selection, from the products we use to the techniques we employ, is made to optimize results.

Eco-Friendly

We're dedicated to employing eco friendly products and water efficient methods that minimize our environmental impact.

Integrity

We value honesty, transparency and doing the right thing. We never oversell service, and our pricing is fair for the work performed.
