Our Insulation Services

Comprehensive insulation solutions for every property type

At InsulMax, we provide a wide range of insulation services for residential and commercial properties. Our team of certified professionals uses high-quality materials and industry-leading techniques to deliver energy-efficient solutions that enhance comfort and reduce utility costs.

Residential Insulation

Our residential insulation services help homeowners improve energy efficiency, reduce utility bills, and enhance comfort throughout their homes. We offer comprehensive solutions for all areas of your home, including:

  • Attic Insulation: Properly insulated attics can significantly reduce heat loss in winter and heat gain in summer.
  • Wall Insulation: We provide insulation solutions for both exterior and interior walls to maintain comfortable indoor temperatures.
  • Basement & Crawl Space Insulation: Prevent moisture issues and improve energy efficiency with our specialized basement and crawl space insulation.
  • Floor Insulation: Keep your floors warm in winter and cool in summer with our floor insulation services.

We use various insulation materials, including fiberglass, cellulose, spray foam, and rigid foam, depending on your home's specific needs and your budget.

Commercial Insulation

Our commercial insulation services are designed to help businesses reduce energy costs, comply with building codes, and create comfortable environments for employees and customers. We work with various commercial properties, including:

  • Office Buildings: Create a comfortable working environment while reducing energy costs.
  • Retail Spaces: Maintain consistent temperatures for customer comfort and protect merchandise.
  • Warehouses: Improve energy efficiency in large spaces and protect stored goods.
  • Manufacturing Facilities: Control temperatures for equipment and processes while reducing energy consumption.

Our commercial insulation solutions include thermal insulation, acoustic insulation, and fire-resistant insulation to meet all your business needs.

Attic Insulation

Proper attic insulation is crucial for maintaining comfortable temperatures in your home and reducing energy costs. Our specialized attic insulation services include:

  • Blown-in Insulation: Quick and effective insulation that fills even the smallest gaps and spaces.
  • Batt Insulation: Traditional insulation that provides consistent coverage for standard attic spaces.
  • Radiant Barrier Installation: Reflects heat away from your home, especially beneficial in warmer climates.
  • Air Sealing: Prevents air leakage and improves the effectiveness of your insulation.

Our experts will assess your attic's current insulation and recommend the best solution to maximize energy efficiency and comfort in your home.

Crawl Space Insulation

Proper crawl space insulation helps prevent moisture problems, improves indoor air quality, and enhances energy efficiency. Our crawl space insulation services include:

  • Vapor Barrier Installation: Prevents moisture from entering your crawl space and causing damage.
  • Floor Joist Insulation: Keeps your floors warm and prevents heat loss through the floor.
  • Crawl Space Encapsulation: Seals your crawl space completely to prevent moisture, pests, and air leaks.
  • Mold Remediation: Removes existing mold and prevents future growth through proper insulation and moisture control.

Our professionals will evaluate your crawl space and recommend the most effective insulation solution for your specific needs.

New Construction Insulation

Properly insulating new construction projects from the start ensures maximum energy efficiency and comfort. Our new construction insulation services include:

  • Pre-Construction Planning: Work with your construction team to design the most effective insulation system.
  • Code-Compliant Installation: Ensure your new building meets or exceeds local building code requirements.
  • Energy-Efficient Solutions: Implement the latest insulation technologies for maximum energy savings.
  • Multi-Step Installation: Coordinate with builders to install insulation at the optimal stages of construction.

We work with homeowners, contractors, and builders to provide high-quality insulation for new homes and commercial buildings.

Insulation Materials We Use

We use high-quality, energy-efficient insulation materials for all our projects

Fiberglass Insulation

Cost-effective and widely used for residential and commercial applications. Available in batts, rolls, and blown-in varieties.

Cellulose Insulation

Eco-friendly option made from recycled paper products. Excellent for retrofitting existing homes and filling irregular spaces.

Spray Foam Insulation

Premium solution that provides superior air sealing and high R-value. Available in open-cell and closed-cell varieties.
