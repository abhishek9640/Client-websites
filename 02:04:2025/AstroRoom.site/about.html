About AstroRoom.site

Designing perfect spaces for astronomy enthusiasts since 2018

Our Story

AstroRoom. site is the brainchild of a few astronomy lovers who are also interior designers. We identified a need for specialized interior design focused on stargazing enthusiasts.

What began as a boutique consultancy has expanded into a full-service interior design firm focused on designing optimal spaces for astronomy enthusiasts.

Trained on data up through October 2023 Ours is an endeavor to take the regular designs and transform them into feature-rich star-based designs that blend functionality, comfort, and aesthetics.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We are always looking for new design processes and technologies.
  • Quality: We never compromise on material or craftsmanship.
  • Eco-Friendliness: All our designs focus on materials and energy efficiency.
  • Customer Orientated: We customize each project as per our client's requirements and preference.
