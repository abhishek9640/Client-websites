About Us

Learn the story behind StarScreen and our passion for outdoor cinema

Our Story

StarScreen was founded in 2018 by a group of film enthusiasts and technology experts who shared a vision: to bring the magic of cinema outdoors. What started as a small operation with a single projector and portable screen has grown into a premier outdoor cinema installation service, serving countless clients across the region.

Our mission is simple: to deliver extraordinary movie experiences under the stars. We believe that cinema is more than just watching a filmâ€”it's about creating memories, bringing people together, and turning ordinary spaces into something magical.

Our Process

1

Consultation

We begin by understanding your requirements, venue constraints, and vision for your outdoor cinema event.

2

Site Assessment

Our team evaluates your space to determine the optimal setup for screen placement, sound coverage, and audience seating.

3

Custom Proposal

Based on our assessment, we provide a detailed proposal including equipment recommendations and pricing options.

4

Installation

Our experienced technicians handle the complete setup, ensuring everything is properly installed and tested.

5

Event Support

A technician remains on-site during your event to ensure smooth operation and address any technical needs.

6

Teardown & Feedback

We handle the complete dismantling of equipment and follow up to ensure your satisfaction with our service.
