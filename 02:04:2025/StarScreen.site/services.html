Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for an outdoor cinema?

The amount of space needed depends on the screen size and audience size. For a typical backyard, we recommend at least 15ft x 20ft of clear space, plus seating space.

What if it rains on the day of my event?

We monitor weather forecasts closely and will contact you to reschedule if necessary. Our flexible booking policies ensure you can adjust your plans for weather events.
