Frequently Asked Questions

How much space do I need for an outdoor cinema?

The required space depends on the screen size and audience size. For a standard backyard setup, we recommend at least 15ft x 20ft of clear space, plus additional room for seating.

What happens if it rains on the day of my event?

We closely monitor weather forecasts and will work with you to reschedule if needed. We also offer flexible booking policies to accommodate weather-related changes.

Do I need a completely dark environment?

While darker environments provide the best viewing experience, our high-brightness projectors can work in partially lit areas. We'll assess your venue and recommend the best setup for your conditions.

How early do you arrive for setup?

We typically arrive 2-3 hours before the scheduled start time to ensure everything is perfectly set up and tested before your guests arrive.
