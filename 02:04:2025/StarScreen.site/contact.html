Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for residential installations and 4-8 weeks for larger events, especially during peak summer months when demand is high.

Do you service my area?

We currently serve clients within a 100-mile radius of Movie City. For locations outside this area, please contact us for special arrangements.

What deposit is required to secure my booking?

A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due one week before the event date.

Can I get a refund if I need to cancel?

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event date will receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 14 days of the event may be eligible for a partial refund or credit toward a future booking.
