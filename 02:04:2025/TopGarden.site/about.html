About TopGarden

Transforming urban spaces into thriving ecosystems

Our Story

Founded in 2012, TopGarden began with a simple mission: to bring nature back to urban environments through innovative roof garden solutions. What started as a small team of passionate gardeners and landscape architects has grown into a leading provider of sustainable rooftop installations across the USA.

Our founder, Michael Green, recognized the untapped potential of urban rooftops as valuable green spaces. With backgrounds in landscape architecture and environmental science, our team developed specialized techniques for creating thriving gardens in these challenging environments.

Over the past decade, we've transformed hundreds of rooftops into vibrant green spaces, helping property owners reduce energy costs, increase property values, and create beautiful retreats in the midst of urban landscapes.

Our Mission & Values

At TopGarden, we're committed to creating sustainable, beautiful, and functional green spaces that benefit both people and the environment. We believe that every roof represents an opportunity to restore a piece of nature to our urban landscape.
