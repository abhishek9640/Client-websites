Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve major metropolitan areas across the United States. Our primary service areas include the Northeast, Midwest, and West Coast regions. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

How quickly can you start a project?

Our current lead time for new projects is typically 4-6 weeks, depending on the season and project scope. For urgent needs or smaller consultations, we can often accommodate faster timelines.

Do you offer site visits before providing a quote?

Yes, we always conduct a thorough site assessment before providing a detailed quote. This initial consultation helps us understand your space and requirements to create an accurate proposal.

Can you provide references from past clients?

Absolutely! We're happy to share references and case studies from previous projects similar to yours. Please request these during your consultation, and we'll provide relevant examples.
