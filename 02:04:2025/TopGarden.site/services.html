Roof Garden Design

Our expert designers create custom roof garden plans that balance aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. We consider your space's unique characteristics and your personal preferences to develop a design that exceeds your expectations.

Our Design Process Includes:

  • Comprehensive site assessment and structural evaluation
  • Custom 3D visualization of your future garden
  • Sustainable plant selection suited to your local climate
  • Irrigation and drainage system design
  • Material selection for pathways, furniture, and decorative elements
  • Budget planning and phasing options

We prioritize eco-friendly design principles, selecting native plants that thrive in your local environment while minimizing water usage and maintenance requirements.

Expert Installation

Our professional installation team brings your roof garden design to life with meticulous attention to detail and adherence to safety standards. We handle all aspects of the installation process to ensure a beautiful, functional, and durable result.

Our Installation Services Include:

  • Waterproofing and root barrier application
  • Drainage system installation
  • Lightweight growing medium placement
  • Plant installation and initial care
  • Irrigation system setup

We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your property while ensuring every element is installed to the highest standards for long-term performance and beauty.

Ongoing Maintenance

Keep your roof garden thriving with our comprehensive maintenance services. Regular care is essential for ensuring your garden's longevity and continued beauty. Our specialized maintenance teams understand the unique requirements of rooftop environments.

Our Maintenance Plans Include:

  • Seasonal plant care and rotation
  • Irrigation system checks and adjustments
  • Fertilization and soil amendment
  • Pest and disease management using eco-friendly methods
  • Pruning and deadheading
  • Drainage system cleaning and maintenance
  • Weather damage assessment and repair

We offer flexible maintenance plans to suit your needs and budget, from monthly visits to quarterly check-ups, ensuring your garden remains a vibrant and enjoyable space throughout the seasons.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can any roof support a garden?

Not all roofs are suitable for gardens without modifications. We conduct structural assessments to determine your roof's capacity and can design lightweight solutions for roofs with limited load-bearing ability.

How much maintenance does a roof garden require?

Maintenance requirements vary based on the garden's design and plant selection. We can create low-maintenance gardens that require minimal attention or provide regular maintenance services for more elaborate installations.

Will a roof garden cause leaks or damage?

When properly designed and installed, roof gardens actually protect and extend the life of your roof membrane. We install specialized waterproofing and root barriers to prevent any water damage or root penetration.

How long does installation take?

Project timelines depend on the garden's size and complexity. Small installations may take 1-2 weeks, while larger projects can take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Are there any tax benefits to installing a roof garden?

Some localities offer tax incentives, rebates, or stormwater fee reductions for green roof installations. We can help identify potential benefits in your area during the consultation process.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, we provide warranties on both our installation work and plant materials. Specific warranty terms are outlined in your project proposal and contract.
