Larvae Management

In the same vein, we also have a larvae management service that kills mosquitoes in the breeding ground so that they don’t become biting adults. This technique is an important part of longer-term mosquito control.

The Process:

  1. Identification: We will inspect your property and identify potential breeding sites such as standing water in bird baths, flower pots, gutters, and natural depressions.
  2. Source Reduction: Wherever possible, we will advise removing standing water by making drainage improvements or by routinely emptying containers.
  3. Larvicide Application: Where water sources can’t be removed, we use environmentally safe larvicides that specifically kill mosquito larvae and not other wildlife or beneficial insects.
  4. Monitoring: Regular follow-ups to ensure continued effectiveness and to address any new breeding sites that may develop.

This approach works best in properties that have water reservoirs, irrigation setup or are near to natural water bodies. This completion not only prevents mosquitoes from completing their life cycle but also greatly reduces adult mosquito populations on your property.

Barrier Sprays

Our barrier treatments are applied to vegetation and surfaces that mosquitoes will land on, forming a protective barrier around your property. Benefits include:

  • Longer-lasting protection (typically 3-4 weeks)
  • Targeted application to minimize environmental impact
  • Effective against multiple species of mosquitoes

Both methods utilize products labeled for mosquitoes that are designed to have minimal impact on beneficial insects. We use trained technicians to apply these treatments in a targeted manner, focusing on areas in which mosquitoes are most likely to rest and breed.
